OVERVIEW OF BIOLOGY

Basic info

Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms, encompasssing their structure, function and growth, evolution, distribution, and taxonomy.
At its core, biology examines the cell, which is the fundamental unit of life. enetics is a crucial field in biology, focusing on the mechanisms of heredity
and variation in organisms. Evolution, driven by natural selection, explains the diversity of life and the adaptation of species to environment.
